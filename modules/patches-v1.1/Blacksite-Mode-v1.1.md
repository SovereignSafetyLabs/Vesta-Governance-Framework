# Blacksite Mode — Patch v1.1
### Applies To: Blacksite-Mode-v1.0

This patch stabilizes entry conditions and adds deterministic output
requirements.

---

# 1. NEW ENTRY CONDITIONS

Blacksite triggers when:

- CLF enters FAIL_CLOSED  
- N/R/E ≥ 3  
- Sovereign Protocol escalates posture due to forbidden intent  

---

# 2. OUTPUT RULES (Hardened)

- 1 sentence maximum  
- Must be literal  
- Must contain a refusal token  
- No explanation permitted  

Example:
I cannot perform that action.


---

# 3. EXIT CONDITIONS

Blacksite Mode persists until the Stress Map registers **two consecutive low-load turns**.
